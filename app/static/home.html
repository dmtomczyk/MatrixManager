How the model works

Matrix Management connects organizations, people, job codes, projects, and assignments into one staffing picture.

Organizations contain employees

An organization is the home structure for your people. Every employee belongs to one organization, even if they work across multiple projects.

Managers lead reporting trees

Employees with a leader job code can manage people. Those direct reports can themselves be managers, giving you a hierarchy inside each organization.

Projects pull people across the org

Projects represent work demand. Assignments connect employees to projects with date ranges and allocation percentages so you can see utilization over time.

Create your first plan

One simple path gets a brand-new workspace into a usable state quickly.

1

Define an organization

Start in People → Organizations and create the team, department, or business unit that your first employees belong to.

2

Set up job codes

In People → Job Codes, define the job codes your employees will use, including whether each one is a leader role.

3

Create employees

In People → Employees, add people, assign them to the organization, select their job codes, and wire up the manager/reporting structure.

4

Create a project

Go to Planning and define the project name, dates, and description so the work demand exists in the system.

5

Assign employees to the project

Open Assignments and connect people to the project with percentages and time windows.

6

Review the outputs

Use Canvas to view structure and Project Dashboard / Planning to review utilization, timelines, and staffing pressure.
